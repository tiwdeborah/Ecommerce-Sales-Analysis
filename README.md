# E-commerce Sales & Customer Transaction Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce transaction and customer sales data to identify purchasing trends, product performance, customer behavior, and revenue drivers. The analysis demonstrates practical business intelligence and reporting skills using Excel, SQL, and Power BI.

## Objectives
•	Evaluate sales and transaction performance.

•	Analyze customer purchasing behavior.

•	Identify top-performing product categories.

•	Generate insights to support business growth and revenue optimization.

## Tools Used
•	Microsoft Excel
•	MySQL
•	Microsoft Power BI

## Key Insights
•	Electronics category contributed the highest share of total revenue.

•	Customer purchasing activity varied significantly across the 4 regions, with the Western region generating the highest revenue (2.8M).

•	Peak sales in the month of May showed increased transaction volume and customer engagement mid-year.

•	Monitor, which was the best-selling product consistently drove revenue growth.

## Deliverables
•	Data cleaning and transformation.

•	SQL business queries.

•	Power BI dashboard reporting.

•	Sales and customer analytics.

•	Business recommendations.
